Developed a financial time-series forecasting model using 2,510 daily gold price observations (2016–2026) to analyze market trends, volatility, and investment behavior. Applied return analysis, rolling volatility estimation, moving-average crossover signals, correlation analysis, ADF stationarity testing, and ARIMA(5,1,0) forecasting to predict future gold prices and evaluate the impact of inflation, monetary policy, and geopolitical events on gold market dynamics.

Tools: Python, Pandas, NumPy, Statsmodels, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Time Series Analysis, Financial Forecasting.
